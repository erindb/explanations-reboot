## [1] "installing webppl"

Counterfactuals

A replication of L&K’s experiments on mTurk, with 20 participants. Only 3 actually met all attention checks, and only 6 came within 0.1 of all attention checks. The rest would have been excluded from the original experiment for providing ratings technically inconsistent with the given premises. In the original online experiment 39/96 participants were excluded for these reasons.

The replication was pretty noisy.
